Making Intake & Referral Management an Advantage

Delivering Experience, Speed & Agility — autonomous, digital, clean route-ready episodes with human in the loop.

The Opportunity

75 referrals arrive every morning by fax, voice, and portal. Each one requires 15–20 minutes of manual review, data entry, gap detection, and outreach. At scale, that's tens of millions of dollars in operational cost — for work that a purpose-built AI agent can do in seconds, with greater accuracy and a complete audit trail.

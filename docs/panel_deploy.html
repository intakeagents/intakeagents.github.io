What we built, how we deploy it in your environment, and how it gets smarter with your data — the complete story for your technology leader.

01 — What We Built
🧩
A Modular Agent Network
Purpose-built for DME referral intake — every component auditable, every decision traceable.
  • AI extraction across fax, voice, and portal channels
  • OWL knowledge graph — 24 deterministic CMS rules
  • Four-layer hallucination defense — no guessing on clinical decisions
  • Automated email + voice outreach via LLM + AI voice agent
  • Full audit trail — every decision with reasoning and confidence score
  • 75 referrals in 60 seconds at $0.032/referral
02 — How We Deploy It
🚀
In Your Environment
Containerized Python — runs on your infrastructure, your cloud, or ours. 8–12 weeks from kickoff to go-live.
  • Connects to your fax gateway, intake portal, or case management system
  • API or file-based integration — no rip-and-replace
  • LLM provider BAA available — PHI never leaves your boundary
  • HIPAA-compliant deployment — data retention policies configurable
  • Your team receives full source code, audit logs, and runbooks
  • Dedicated support through pilot and go-live
03 — How It Gets Smarter
📈
With Your Data
Every processed episode is a signal. The agent learns your payers, your patterns, your edge cases.
  • ICD patterns tuned to your most common payer combinations
  • Knowledge graph extended with your carrier-specific rules
  • Confidence thresholds calibrated to your escalation tolerance
  • Month 3+: accuracy improves as it learns your referral patterns
  • New payer rules added without code changes — KG config update only
  • Feedback loop from human review queue feeds next-cycle improvement
📋
25-Field Episode Record
Patient, claim, ICD-10, HCPCS, physician, adjuster, delivery address, auth ref, transportation, language — structured and validated on every referral.
🧠
Large Language Model + OWL KG
AI handles extraction and clinical reasoning. Knowledge graph handles deterministic CMS compliance. Neither alone is sufficient — together they're deployable.
📞
Live Voice Intake + Outreach
AI voice agent answers the intake line 24/7. Outbound calls auto-triggered after 24hrs with no email response. 84-second average call, full transcript logged.
🛡️
Four-Layer Hallucination Defense
Document cross-reference → KG validation → confidence threshold → human gate. When confidence drops below 80%, the agent stops and routes to a specialist.
